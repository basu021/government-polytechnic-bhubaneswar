Principal's Message

Diploma education is a perfect combination between knowledge and skill .Pursuing diploma education provides students avenues for higher education, employment and self employment. Government Polytechnic, Bhubaneswar is a premier institute where we focus on the holistic development and progress of our students. Education doesn't mean accumulation of information Only. Education is the right way of perception and implementation of the knowledge. Government Polytechnic, Bhubaneswar offers the opportunity for young individuals to learn the skills that paves their way to a brighter tomorrow.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a premier Diploma Institution in the Eastern India by transforming the learners into competent technocrats by imparting technical education in multiple disciplines.

Our Mission

1. Focus on quality teaching & learning by providing better academic environment for both faculties and students.
2. Develop professionally and technically skilled students who work for betterment of the society.
3. Nurture positive attitude and social values amongst the students and staff.

Core Values

  1. Team Work
  2. Transparency
  3. Dicipline
  4. Social Awareness
  5. Empathy
